How to Set Up Zoom for Music Lessons

Prepare Your Computer

Ensure your computer is fully charged and has a stable internet connection. Set up in a quiet, distraction-free environment.

Check Your Microphone

Use a USB microphone or your device's built-in mic. Test your audio in Zoom to ensure clear sound quality.

Adjust Your Camera

Position your camera so the teacher can see you and your instrument clearly. Make sure you have good lighting.

Optimize Zoom Settings

Enable "Original Sound" in Zoom to improve audio quality for music. Turn off background noise suppression.
